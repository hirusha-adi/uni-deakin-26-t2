1.12 Prototyping
Learning Goals
By the end of this topic, you should be able to:

Understand what a prototype is.
Learn how to use tools such as Adobe XD or Figma for prototyping.
What is a Prototype?
A prototype is a tangible representation of your design that allows you to visualise how users will interact with your screens and navigate through your product. Turning your sketches into prototypes offers several benefits:

User Interaction: Observe how users interact with your design and navigate through your product. This helps in understanding usability and the effectiveness of your design.
Testing and Feedback: Conduct tests to identify missing actions or areas for improvement based on feedback from users and testers.
To create a prototype, start with your wireframes or sketches and use design tools such as Adobe XD or Figma to develop interactive versions of your design.




The final step is to connect screens using interactive hotspots to create a functional prototype.

Activities
