What does Sketch mean?
Sketching refers to the process of quickly generating ideas through rough drawings or outlines. It's a fast way to visualise concepts and explore different design possibilities before moving on to more detailed work.

Why sketching and drawing are super-important skills for every UX designer
Image Source: https://agentestudio.com/blog/how-to-wireframe-an-app
Sketching allows you to quickly articulate and communicate your thoughts and ideas by getting them out of your head and onto paper for validation. If you jump straight into development or design without sketching, you may end up wasting time fixing or reworking concepts. By sketching your ideas first, it's easier to share and discuss them with your team, which can save a lot of time and effort in the long run, making it more efficient for the business.
