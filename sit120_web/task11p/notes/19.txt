1.9 User Experience Design
Learning Goals
By the end of this topic, you should be able to:

Understand the importance of user experience
Learn how UX can improve your project
Apply user experience methodologies
What is user experience?
User Experience is how your product is used by your users. Does your product solve their problems? And do they use your product effectively? Everything is related to user experience.

What methods can I use for user experience?

Image Source: https://www.nngroup.com/articles/ux-research-cheat-sheet/
Discover: Search for information on the Internet or in books to gather general information.
Explore: Conduct research and surveys on your competitors, and analyse the pros and cons of your products.
Test: Test your prototype with users.
Listen and Validate: Collect feedback from your testers to improve your UX and the workflow of the product.
